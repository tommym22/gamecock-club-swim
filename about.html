About The team

Welcome! Gamecock Club Swim is an organization for active, retired, or new athletes interested in competitive swimming. We offer practices for all skill levels, Monday through Thursday. Each semester, the team travels to competitions, swimming against, but also interacting with other collegiate club swim teams. GCS is a blast and we hope to see you soon!

Executive Board Members

President

I'm Beth Warren, and I'm a Marketing and HR Management double major from Glendale, IL. I started swimming competitively when I was 4 and thought I was done with the sport after high school. Luckily, the president my freshman year was a family friend and I met people who convinced me to join. I've served as a Social Media Co-Chair, Vice President, and now President. My leadership in GCS has led me to serve on the College Club Swimming Advisory Board. Outside of the pool, I'm in the Honors College, a service sorority (ESA), and am the secretary of the Sport Club Executive Board. I love arts and crafts, sharks, and reading!

I have swam my whole life, and going into college I wanted to join the club swim team to make friends and stay in shape.
