FAQS

General

What is Gamecock Club Swim?
A: Check out our about page to learn more.

How do I know if this is right for me?
A: At the beginning of each semester, we offer a “trial period” where you can swim with the team free of charge.

Can anyone join?
A: Gamecock Club Swim is open to everyone, regardless of skill level.

How do I join?
A: Follow us on Garnet Gate to receive announcements about registration. You can also use the links in the menu above.

Is there a fee to join?
A: New members have a fee of $175 for their first semester. Returning members only have to pay $100 per semester.

Practices

Do I need to have swam with a high school or club team to practice?
A: Nope! The only requirement is you know how to swim.

Are practices super hard? Too easy?
A: Our practice chair(s) write multiple sets so you can swim at the difficulty you feel comfortable with.

Can anyone join?
A: Gamecock Club Swim is open to everyone, regardless of skill level.

When is practice?
A: We offer 4 practices a week (Mon-Thur) from 7:30-8:30 pm. Join the calendar (featured on the home page) to get notified of any schedule changes.

Do I need to go to every practice?
A: You can go to as many or as little practices as you’d like.

Meets and Competitions

Do you have competitions?
A: Yes! We aim to have 4 meets a semester.

Where are the meets located?
A: We have usually have competitions from North Carolina all the way down to Florida.

How do I sign up for meets?
A: Roughly two weeks before the competition, an email will be sent out with an interest form.

Does everyone get to travel?
A: Everyone is eligible to fill out the meet interest form. However, due to limited available space at competitions, people are chosen on a first come first serve basis. Everyone can swim at home meets.

Do I need to go to every practice?
A: You can go to as many or as little practices as you’d like.
